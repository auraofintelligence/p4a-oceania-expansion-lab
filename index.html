P4A Oceania Expansion Lab

A public workbench for pushing the Purple Party for Australia idea into an Oceania-scale civic moonshot: care receipts, local-first compute, disaster resilience, transparent ledgers, culture, law memory, Brisbane 2032 mobilisation and space-facing imagination.

Marked experiment

No party or Oceania organisation is being claimed here. Inside that boundary, the ambition is allowed to run: prototype the language, map the architecture, expose the gates and make the machinery visible before it asks for trust.

Public status: exploratory drafting site. The boundary is clear so the experiment can go bigger without pretending it is already authorised.

From P4A to P4O

The blue layer opens.

The existing P4A site says the work is a proposed movement and drafting project. This Oceania lab keeps that honesty while letting the scale breathe: a regional civic interface that can hold care economics, disaster response, public ledgers, legal memory, culture and future infrastructure in one inspectable public map.

Copy the useful civic scaffold, not the authority. Authority is earned locally, but imagination can move first and leave clear receipts behind it.
