Public status

What this site is not.

Clear boundaries make the experiment safer. They also make it easier for future humans, lawyers, community reviewers and agents to know what needs checking.

  • Not registered: P4A is treated here as a proposed movement and drafting project, not a registered political party.
  • Not Oceania authority: The lab does not speak for Oceania, Pacific peoples, Australia, Aotearoa New Zealand, First Nations, tangata whenua or any government.
  • Not adopted policy: Every proposal is exploratory unless a future public record marks it as adopted by a legitimate body.
  • Not legal or electoral advice: Registration, donations, disclosure, promoter statements, membership and campaigning need live legal review in each jurisdiction.
  • Not medical advice: Health, sauna, HBOT, toxicology or biological claims from the source memo are not programmes here and need proper medical evidence and governance.
  • Not financial advice: C-Hours are described as a draft contribution-receipt idea only. Nothing is issued, sold, redeemed or promised.

Ledger
