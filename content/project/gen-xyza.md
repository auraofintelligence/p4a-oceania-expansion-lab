# Gen XYZA Participant Notes

Audience posture:

- speak to citizens of Oceania as self-sovereign entities
- treat young people as operators, builders, digital custodians and future treaty witnesses
- write for people who already carry agency, skill and refusal power
- offer doors, quests, receipts, forks and invitations
- ask what people will build, verify, protect, heal, code, repair, teach or challenge

Tone:

- vivid
- direct
- respectful
- high-trust
- high-agency
- moonshot without fake certainty

Avoid:

- patronising warnings
- school-principal language
- subject/object framing
- gatekeeping voice that treats participants as subjects
- shrinking the idea to satisfy imagined critics
